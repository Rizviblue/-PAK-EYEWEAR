Shipping Policy

We offer reliable shipping services to deliver your online orders. Our shipping process is simple and efficient.

Shipping Methods

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery within 5 to 7 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Delivery within 2 to 3 business days.
  • Overnight Shipping: Next-day delivery on orders placed before 2 PM.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on the weight and destination of your order.

Return Policy

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of the delivery date.

How to Return an Item

  • Contact our customer service at support@fashionhub.com to initiate a return.
  • Package the item securely in its original packaging with all tags.
  • Ship the item to the address provided by the customer service representative.

Return Shipping Costs

The customer is responsible for paying shipping costs for returned items, unless the return is due to a defective product or a shipping error.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5 to 7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment.
